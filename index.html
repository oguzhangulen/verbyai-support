Frequently Asked Questions

How does VerbyAI determine my level?

When you first open the app, you can take a quick placement test. VerbyAI evaluates your answers and assigns you a CEFR level from A1 (beginner) to C2 (proficient). All exercises are then tailored to your level.

Why do I see different exercises every day?

VerbyAI generates fresh, AI-powered content daily. Vocabulary words, grammar topics, reading passages, and phrases change every day to maximize your learning.

How does the Scan feature work?

Point your camera at any English text — a book, menu, or sign. VerbyAI uses OCR to extract the words and provides meanings, pronunciations, and example sentences for each word.

Is my data stored securely?

Yes. Your learning data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect or share any personal information.

How can I reset my progress?

Go to Profile > Settings and use the reset option to start fresh. Please note that this action cannot be undone.
